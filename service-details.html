High Visibility & Reflective Solutions

Engineered for demanding duty cycles, our garments maintain luminance and retro‑reflective performance after repeated washing and daily abrasion. Colourfast fabrics and certified tape placement ensure visibility in low light, rain and dust.

From small maintenance crews to national logistics fleets we provide sizing guidance, branding options and rapid fulfilment. Local sourcing enables consistent quality while supporting South African manufacturing strength.

Care & Maintenance

Simple steps extend garment life and preserve visibility performance.

Gentle Wash

Cold or mild cycle. Avoid harsh bleaching agents to protect colour intensity.

Air Dry

Line dry away from direct high heat sources to maintain tape adhesion.

Inspect Regularly

Replace garments showing reflective degradation to sustain compliance.
